What are the important things I should know about riding a bike?

Always wear a helmet that fits well to protect your head. If riding at night, use front and rear lights along with reflectors. Wear bright, reflective clothing, and consider gloves and knee/elbow pads for extra protection.

What are some key things I should know when learning how to swim?

Always swim in designated areas and under supervision, especially if you’re a beginner. Know how to float, tread water, and recognize potential dangers like strong currents. Use goggles to protect your eyes and consider wearing a swim cap for better hydrodynamics.
